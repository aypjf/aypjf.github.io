Jingfu Peng

 

I am an Assistant Professor at the Yau Mathematical Sciences Center (YMSC), Tsinghua University. My research focuses on statistical learning theory and machine learning theory.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at YMSC, working with Yuhong Yang. I earned my Ph.D. in Statistics from Renmin University of China in 2023 under the supervision of Professor Yang Li. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree in Statistics from Renmin University of China in 2018.
